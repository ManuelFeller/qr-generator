You can easily extract the values from your map service of choice.
Just center the map at the desired location and copy them from the URL.
OpenStreetMap and Google Maps both use Latitude first, followed by Longitude.

In the following two examples the Latitude is 49.9764195 and the Longitude is 8.0560682:
https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=14/49.9764195/8.0560682
https://www.google.com/maps/@49.9764195,8.0560682,14z

Note: if you enter a comma instead of a dot it will be automatically converted, so no worries ;)
